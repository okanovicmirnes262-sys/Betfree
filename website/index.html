How it works

Three steps between you
and your first bet-free month.

01

Face the real number

A 2-minute, brutally honest test shows your risk level and what betting will cost you over the next 5 years. Most people never forget that number.

02

Get your quit plan

Day 1: self-exclusion from every bookmaker, step by step. Week 1: blockers and card removal. Every day after: tools that keep you on the path.

03

Watch freedom compound

Your streak ticks up every second — and so does the money you didn't lose. Milestones, daily motivation and a goal to spend it on something real.

Built for the hard moments

Everything a quitting gambler
actually needs.

⚡

Panic button

An urge lasts 15–20 minutes. Sixty seconds of guided breathing, plus your own reasons for quitting — shown exactly when your brain starts lying to you.

💶

Live savings counter

Every second without a bet, your counter grows. Set a goal — a trip, a debt paid off — and watch the progress bar fill with money that stays yours.

☀️

Daily motivation

A new message every day, tuned to your streak — from surviving day one to owning year one. Special celebrations on milestone days.

📓

Urge journal

Log every urge you beat and see your patterns — most urges come from the same few triggers. Daily mood check-ins show your recovery on a chart.

🤝

Anonymous community

A wall of people fighting the same fight, nickname-only. Share a win at 2am and someone on day 200 sends you strength. No comments, no trolls.

🛡️

Risky-hours shield

Match nights? Payday? Tell BetFree when urges usually hit, and the app meets you there with your plan front and centre.
